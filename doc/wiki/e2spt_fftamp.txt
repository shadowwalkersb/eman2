{{{

usage: prog <Volume file> [options]
	This program writes out the 3D image of the amplitudes of the Fourier transform of a subvolume or a stack of subvolumes.

}}}


||option||type||description||
||-h, --help|| ||show this help message and exit||
||--version|| ||show program's version number and exit||
||--input||str||default=None. Filename of 3-D image or stack of 3-D images whose FFT amplitudes you want to see.||
||--ppid||int||Default=-1. Set the PID of the parent process, used for cross platform PPID||
||--verbose, -v||int||verbose level [0-9], higher number means higher level of verboseness||
